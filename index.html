The Dark Side of the Soo

We are a Pink Floyd tribute band from Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, dedicated to recreating the sound, vision, and spirit of one of the greatest rock bands of all time. Built with care, humility, and attention to detail, our show aims to deliver an experience as close as possible to a full-scale Floyd production—scaled for intimate venues.

Lighting & Visuals

Synchronized lighting and projected videos run in perfect time with the music, creating the unmistakable atmosphere fans expect.

Equipment & Setup

We provide a self-contained show including:

  • PA system (ideal up to ~150)
  • Backline instruments
  • Lighting rig (time-coded). 8 RGB Led Par and Smoke Machine
  • Video projection & screen

A large-concert feel—practical and affordable for bars and small rooms.

What’s in a name?

“The Dark Side of the Soo” nods to the classic prism and to Sault Ste. Marie’s nickname—“the Soo.” We’re available across Ontario and Québec, and we love smaller rooms where we can be an arm’s length from the audience.
